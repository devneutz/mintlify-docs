<Note>
  This page is for **volunteers**. If you're a club admin looking to create
  an event, see [Creating an Event](/en/guides/creating-an-event) instead.
</Note>

You don't need an account to help out — just your name and email.

## Finding an event

Your club shares a link with you directly (e.g. by chat, email, or a QR
code) — there's no public directory of clubs. That link takes you either to:

- Your **club's page**, listing every currently published event, or
- A specific **event's page** directly (sometimes with a team already
  preselected, if you got a team-specific link).

## Signing up

On the event page, shifts are grouped by day and show how many spots are
still free (or **Full** / **Past** if not). For any open shift:

1. Click **Sign up**.
2. Enter your **name** and **email**.
3. If your club uses teams, optionally pick one from the **Team** dropdown
   (it may already be preselected from the link you followed — you can
   still change it).
4. Submit.

<Info>
  Signing up for a shift that overlaps another shift you're already in
  (same event, same email) isn't allowed — back-to-back shifts are fine.
</Info>

## After you sign up

You'll get a confirmation email with:

- The shift's details.
- A **manage link**, unique to your signup, that lets you view, cancel, or
  request deletion of your data at any time — see
  [Managing Your Signup](/en/volunteers/managing-your-signup).

Your name and email are used only to organize this event, per the club's
[privacy notice](/en/volunteers/managing-your-signup#privacy--your-data),
linked from the signup form.
