<Note>
  This page is for **volunteers**. You don't create an account with
  ShowUpFor — your confirmation email's manage link *is* your access.
</Note>

## Opening your signup

Every confirmation (and reminder) email you receive includes a personal
**manage link**. Opening it shows:

- Your signup's status (**Confirmed** or **Cancelled**).
- The event, task, date, time, location, and team (if any).
- The name and email you signed up with.

<Warning>
  If the link says it's invalid or expired, either the link itself is
  wrong, or your data has already been deleted (e.g. after cancelling, or
  after the club's retention period passed). Contact the club directly
  using the email on their [privacy notice](#privacy--your-data).
</Warning>

## Cancelling your signup

Click **Cancel signup** and confirm. This is available any time before the
shift starts — once it has started, cancelling is no longer possible and
you'd need to contact the club directly. Cancelling frees your spot for
someone else and sends you a cancellation email.

## Deleting your data

Click **Delete my data** and confirm. This immediately and permanently
erases your name and email from the signup (also cancelling it if it was
still active) — the club keeps only an anonymous headcount. This can't be
undone, and your manage link stops working afterward.

<Tip>
  Even without asking, your data is deleted automatically a set number of
  days after the event ends (set by the club, commonly 30 days) — deleting
  it yourself just does it sooner.
</Tip>

## Privacy & your data

Every club has its own privacy notice (linked from the signup form and from
your manage page) explaining what's collected, why, for how long, and how
to reach the club directly. Your data is only ever used to run the event
you signed up for.
