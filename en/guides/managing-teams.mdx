---
title: "Managing Teams"
description: "Teams and departments for reporting and pre-filled links"
---

Teams (or departments) let you attribute signups to a group within your
club — useful for clubs organized into juniors, women's teams, or
departments that each want to see their own turnout.

<Note>
  Teams are a free feature — available on every plan.
</Note>

## Where to manage them

Under **Settings → Teams / departments**, you can:

- **Add a team** by name (2–100 characters).
- **Remove a team** — existing signups keep their data but lose the team
  attribution; nothing about the signup itself is deleted.

## What teams affect

- **At signup**: if your club has any teams, the public signup form shows an
  optional team dropdown (volunteers can also leave it unset).
- **Team links**: each event gets one share link per team (found under
  **Team links** on the event page) that preselects that team — handy for
  posting in a specific team's group chat. Volunteers can still change the
  team after opening the link.
- **On the event page**: admins can set or change any signup's team from the
  shift's volunteer list.
- **In statistics**: the event's Statistics page breaks down shifts per
  team, and shows a "No team" bucket for anyone unassigned.
