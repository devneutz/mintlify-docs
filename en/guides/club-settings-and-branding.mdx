Your club's basic details live under **Settings** in the admin nav.

| Field | Notes |
| --- | --- |
| Club name | Shown throughout the club's public pages and emails. |
| Contact email | Used as the reply address for club emails and shown on the club's privacy notice. |
| Logo URL | A link to a hosted image (`https://…`), e.g. the logo already on your club website. |
| Accent color <Tooltip tip="Requires the Pro plan">Pro</Tooltip> | Replaces the default ShowUpFor color across your club's public pages. |

<Note>
  Your **club identifier** (the slug in every URL, e.g. `fc-alpha`) is shown
  here too but can't be changed — it's baked into your club's links, QR
  codes, and login.
</Note>

## Accent color is a Pro feature

<Info>
  **Pro feature.** On the Free plan, the color picker is locked and your
  club's public pages use the default ShowUpFor color. Upgrading under
  [Billing & Plans](/en/guides/billing-and-plans) unlocks a custom color for
  your club.
</Info>

If your club already had a custom color set before a downgrade, it keeps
rendering — only *changing* it requires Pro.
