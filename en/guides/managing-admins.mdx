title: "Managing Admins"
description: "Invite or remove co-admins, and admin seat limits"
---

Admins are the Clerk-authenticated users who can manage your club — every
club-admin action requires being a member of the club's admin group.

## Inviting an admin

Under **Admins** in the top nav (labeled **Benutzerverwaltung** in the
German UI):

1. Enter the person's email under **Invite an admin** and submit.
2. They receive an invitation email with a link to set up their account (or
   sign in with an existing one).
3. Until accepted, they show up in the list with a **pending** badge; you
   can revoke a pending invitation at any time.

## Removing an admin

Click **Remove** next to a member's name and confirm. This revokes their
access immediately.

<Warning>
  A club must always keep at least one admin — removing the last one is
  blocked.
</Warning>

## Admin seats

<Info>
  **Pro feature.** The Free plan includes **2 admin seats** per club; Pro
  removes the limit entirely. This is enforced when you send an invite — if
  your club is at its seat limit, the invite is rejected with an upgrade
  message rather than sent. See [Billing & Plans](/en/guides/billing-and-plans)
  to upgrade.
</Info>
