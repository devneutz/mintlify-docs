title: "Managing Clubs (Platform Operators)"
description: "Create, invite admins for, and delete clubs on your deployment"

<Note>
  This page is for **platform operators**: the people running the whole
  ShowUpFor deployment, not a single club. If you're a club admin, see the
  **Guides** tab instead.
</Note>

Operators are a separate population from club admins — being an operator on
the platform gives you no access to any club's admin area, and vice versa.

## Signing in

Operators sign in at `/{locale}/platform/login`. Access is membership in a
dedicated operator group on the deployment — ask an existing operator to
add you if you don't have it yet.

## Viewing clubs

The **Clubs** page lists every club on the deployment, with its admin
count, event count, and creation date. Click a club to open its detail
page.

## Creating a club

From **Clubs**, click **New club** and fill in:

| Field | Notes |
| --- | --- |
| Club name | Shown throughout the club's pages. |
| Club identifier (URL) | Lowercase letters, digits and hyphens only, e.g. `fc-muster`. Part of every link the club will ever have — **cannot be changed later**. |
| Club contact email | The club's own contact address (privacy notice, reply-to). |
| First admin email | Who gets invited to administer the new club. |

Submitting creates the club and sends the first admin an invitation email
immediately.

## Managing an existing club

A club's detail page (under **Clubs**) shows its admins and any pending
invitations, and lets you:

- **Resend an invitation** if the first admin hasn't accepted yet.
- **Delete the club** — permanently removes it along with every event,
  shift, and signup it has. This can't be undone and requires confirming
  the club's name.

## What operators don't do

Operators don't manage a club's events, shifts, or day-to-day settings —
that's the club admin's job, covered in the **Guides** tab. An operator can
resend a first-admin invite or delete a club outright, but has no way to
edit a club's content directly.
