title: "Introduction"
description: "What ShowUpFor is and how club admins use it"
---

ShowUpFor is volunteer shift planning for sports clubs. Instead of a paper
sign-up sheet pinned to the clubhouse wall, your club gets a shareable web
page where volunteers pick a shift with just their name and email — no
account needed.

This documentation is for **club admins**: the people who create events,
open shifts, and keep track of who's signed up.

## How it fits together

## Signing in

Your club admin area lives at:

```
/{locale}/verein/{your-club-slug}/admin
```

on your club's ShowUpFor deployment (for example `.../verein/fc-alpha/admin`).
If you don't have access yet, ask an existing admin of your club to invite
you — see [Inviting other admins](/guides/administering-an-event#inviting-other-admins).
